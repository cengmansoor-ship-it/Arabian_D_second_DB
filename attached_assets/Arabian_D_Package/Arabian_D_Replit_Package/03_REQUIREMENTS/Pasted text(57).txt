Important Business Logic:
When sale is created:
• Unit status becomes Sold
• Sale ledger created
• Project sold count increases automatically via report query

When payment is received:
• Insert sale payment
• Update sale remaining balance
• If fully paid, mark sale Fully Paid
• Create receipt printable page
• Add audit log

When rental contract is created:
• Unit status becomes Rented
• Contract ledger created

When rent payment is received:
• Payment must be assigned to exact month or date period
• Remaining rent must auto-calculate
• Add audit log

When contract ends:
• Calculate used days/months
• Calculate final payable rent
• Mark contract ended
• Unit becomes Available unless sold or blocked
